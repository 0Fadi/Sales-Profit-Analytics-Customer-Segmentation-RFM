# 📊 Sales & Profit Analytics (Customer Segmentation - RFM)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a deep dive into business performance, analyzing over **$2M in Sales** and **$283K in Profit**. Beyond standard tracking, I implemented **RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)** to segment **2,501 customers** into actionable groups, allowing for targeted marketing and retention strategies.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Power BI:** Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive dashboarding.
* **Excel:** Initial data cleaning and structuring of the **9,994 orders** dataset.
* **Analysis Technique:** RFM Segmentation to classify customers (Champions, Loyal, Potential Loyalists, At Risk).

---

## 🚀 Key Insights (The "So-What" of the Data)

### 👥 Customer Segmentation (RFM)
* **Champions:** Identified the top-tier customers contributing **$141K** to total profit.
* **At Risk:** Spotted **554 customers** who haven't purchased recently, accounting for only **$6K** in profit, requiring immediate re-engagement campaigns.
* **Potential Loyalists:** A large segment of **747 customers** represents the biggest growth opportunity.

### 📈 Sales & Profit Trends
* **Growth:** Profit shows a steady upward trend from **$49K in 2012** to **$81K in 2015**.
* **Seasonality:** **Q4** is the strongest period, contributing **38.24%** of total annual sales.
* **Product Performance:** **Technology** is the most profitable category, while certain products in **Furniture** show lower margins due to high discounts.

### 🌍 Territory Analysis
* Identified geographic "hotspots" and underperforming regions (e.g., Texas, Ohio) where losses were noted, suggesting a need for pricing or discount strategy review in those areas.

---

## 💡 Recommendations (Action Plan)
* **Loyalty Programs:** Reward the **Champions** segment with exclusive offers to maintain their high lifetime value.
* **Retention Strategy:** Launch email marketing or special discounts for the **554 "At Risk"** customers to prevent churn.
* **Discount Optimization:** Review discount levels in the **Furniture** category, as data shows a correlation between high discounts and profit dips in specific territories.
